What is a Feeder?

The Feeder

The feeder is believed to be one of the lowest form of a gamer. This is a gamer who intentionally "feeds" the enemy team and is overall the downfall of his own team. Another case is when a player is just so trash at a game that people call him out for being a feeder because he is feeding the enemy team the win. However, we at Feeder Inc. are striving to redefine what "Feeder" means in online gaming and it begins with you.

What "Feeder" Means to Us

To us here at Feeder Inc, a feeder is potential. A feeder is a player who is on the lower side of the online gaming community and hopefully wants to change their gameplay for the better. That's where we come in. Feeder Inc. FEEDS the online community goods and services which will help those "feeders" become high tier players and eventually redefine the word "feeder."
